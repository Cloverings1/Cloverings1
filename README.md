## What I am building

Krevo is where I spend most of my time. The product is simple on purpose: upload, organize, access, and move files without making storage feel like a second job.

The work underneath is less simple. I care about upload throughput, download reliability, quiet UI, billing that does not surprise people, and release paths that can be trusted after they leave my laptop.

## How I work

I like small loops: find the broken edge, reproduce it, fix it, verify it, ship it. I am still close enough to the fundamentals that I read the docs carefully, and far enough into real products that I know the docs are only the start.

I am usually working across:

- Web apps with Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind
- Storage, auth, billing, and deployment flows
- macOS app work in Swift
- Product copy and UI that says less but does more

## Current focus

- Making Krevo faster and easier to trust
- Tightening the Mac app and web dashboard into one coherent workflow
- Building in public without making noise for its own sake
